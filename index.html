What the Web Industry Can Do to Help

Communication

Developers make sure important information can be accessed anywhere, anytime, from desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.

Connection

Websites and applications like Google Hangouts and Houseparty help keep people connected to eachother.

Answering Questions

With so much uncertainty, the web industry must work with medical organizations and government officials to communicate with those who have questions and concerns.

Entertainment

While everyone is at home, websites, social media, and online entertainment are being consumed more than ever before.
